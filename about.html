History

Founded in 2021 by Caroline, Evelyne and Valentine Kimani in memory of their late mother, a devoted Presbyterian Church Elder and a Woman's Guild member, the Rose Cancer Foundation was founded to create awareness, provide education and preventative resources on gynaecological cancers for women. As reflected in the name, the foundation came about after Rose passed away from a gynacoligical cancer.

Mission

The mission of the cancer foundation is to create awareness, provide education and preventative resources on gynaelogical cancers for women.

Daughters
