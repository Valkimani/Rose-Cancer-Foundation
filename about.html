History

Founded in 2021 by Caroline, Evelyne and Valentine in memory of their late mother, a devoted Presbyterian Church Elder and a Woman's Guild member, the Rose Cancer Foundation was founded to create awareness, provide education and preventative resources to women on gynecological cancers for women. As reflected in the name, the foundation came about after Rose passed away from a gynacoligical cancer.

Mission

Our mission is to create awareness, provide education and preventative resources to women on gynecological cancers.

Founders

In memory of

The late Elder Rose. Though not physically here anymore, her love and light live on forever.

Daughters

Roses' daughters run the foundation. Born in Kenya and currently residing in Atlanta, GA.
